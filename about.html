Tiaser Square

Tiaser Square is a Colombo-Brazilian restaurant, whose goal is to show some Brazilian and Colombian foods to Toronto city. It was built with the efforts of two Brazilian and one Colombian guys to provide high quality and tasty food from these beautiful cultures. Moreover, to provide the best culinary and service that characterizes our countries.
